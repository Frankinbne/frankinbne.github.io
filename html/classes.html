WHAT WE DO

Welcome to ML Strength, where we offer a diverse range of group fitness classes to cater to all fitness levels and interests. Our expert instructors are dedicated to providing a supportive and motivating environment to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Whether you're looking for a high-energy cardio workout or a more gentle and restorative session, we have a class for you. We also offer a range of dance-inspired classes like Body Jam, Body Vive, World Dance, and Hilo, as well as cycling classes like Cycle and Cycle Extreme.

At ML Strength, we believe that fitness should be fun, challenging, and accessible to everyone. So, no matter what your fitness level or experience, we have a class that's right for you. Join us today and start your fitness journey with us!
